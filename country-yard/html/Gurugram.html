Welcome to Courtyard Gurugram Downtown

A 5-star business hotel in Gurgaon

Check into Courtyard By Marriott Gurugram Downtown and discover an upscale business hotel centrally located in the city center of Gurugram, Haryana, India. From our hotel, access popular Gurugram attractions like DLF Cyber City, Golf Course Road and Delhi. Passionate about hospitality, our downtown Gurugram hotel is a welcoming respite for business and leisure travelers in Gurugram, the Millennium City of India. Recharge in beautifully appointed, well-equipped hotel rooms and suites in Gurugram with LED TVs, high-speed Wi-Fi, marble bathrooms, plush featherbeds and 24-hour room service. Host flawless corporate and social events in sophisticated spaces enhanced by custom catering. After exploring Gurugram, refresh in our outdoor pool or treat yourself to delicious international cuisine at our restaurant Courtyard Grill or Downtown Kitchen and Bar. Soothe your senses with a treatment at our spa. Courtyard Gurugram is the perfect hotel for your visit to Haryana, with easy access to Indira Gandhi International Airport.
