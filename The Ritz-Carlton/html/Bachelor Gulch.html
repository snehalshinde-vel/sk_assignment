Welcome to The Ritz-Carlton, Bachelor Gulch

Beaver Creek, Colorado

Consistently recognized as one of North Americas top hotels and Colorado’s premier luxury ski resort, The Ritz-Carlton, Bachelor Gulch is nestled among the majestic Rocky Mountains. The year-round destination resort offers limitless outdoor adventure and amenities featuring ski-in, ski-out access in winter and summer mountain concierge services, as well as an array of sophisticated dining options. The resort offers access to the finest Vail Valley and Beaver Creek golf courses, and a luxurious 21,000 square-foot spa and fitness center with 19 treatment rooms, a co-ed rock-lined grotto, an integrated wellness program, and both Pilates and yoga studios.

Luxury in Beaver Creek

Enveloped by the natural beauty of Beaver Creek, this luxury resort weaves its surroundings into nearly every hotel experience. Regional microbrews are served at Buffalos gastropub, activated charcoal sourced from Colorado mines is used in the spa’s signature Miner’s Mineral Mud Wrap and resort activities pay tribute to the great outdoors, from ski-in/ski-out access during the winter to hiking and kayaking in the warmer months.

Elevated Accommodations

Drawing inspiration from the mountains and the earth, our final refurbishments offer elevated room and suite interiors, evoking a natural feel that brings the organic elements of the mountains indoors.

A Spacious Stay Awaits

With the option of many unique designs and layouts, there is an accommodation for every guest’s desires in one of our three-bedroom penthouses. Whether spending the holiday season with family or planning the ultimate winter vacation with friends, our penthouses will make you feel right at home while still providing all of the luxurious resort amenities.

Mountain Modern Design

Refreshed public spaces compliment the great outdoors with a modern, yet rustic ambience. The Club Lounge, The Great Room, the Lobby and our new private dining space exhibit a luxe overhaul that incorporate organic textures and earthy tones, in addition to its mountain modern design.

Refreshed Dining Experiences

Our multi-million dollar renovation began with the opening of Sakaba, a premier sushi lounge rooted in Japanese tradition providing a sophisticated dining experience to our guests. Our enhanced Mountain View Terrace is the heart of our outdoor experience. Our enhanced outdoor terraces including Fireside Bar, WYLD Terrace and Bar, and Buffalos Terrace and Bar redefine the après experience allowing for seamless movement between our resort and the mountain.

Creating Meetings

Surrounded by the natural beauty of Beaver Creek, The Ritz-Carlton, Bachelor Gulch offers the ideal setting for meetings, executive retreats, incentive trips and training sessions. Events can be hosted indoors, where 15,000 sq. ft. of meeting space includes features such as Juliet balconies and floor-to-ceiling windows, or outside with venues such as the valley-view Skiers Bridge. The resort is a complete meeting destination with a host of specialty services including a photography concierge, wellness workshops and team golf or ski outings.

A Dream Wedding Destination

This Beaver Creek resort offers a storybook setting, located amongst the rolling mountains that paint the horizon. Here, couples can wed on a grassy terrace overlooked by the White River National Forest, in a rustic cabin from the early 1900s or in a grand ballroom where wrought-iron chandeliers shed a warm glow on the damask-inlaid walls.
