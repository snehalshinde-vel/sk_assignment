Welcome to The Ritz-Carlton, Atlanta

At The Ritz-Carlton, Atlanta you will find an exceptional retreat in the heart of downtown Atlanta. Whether you are traveling for important business or planning an adventure-filled weekend retreat, our luxury Atlanta hotel offers world-class service, an ideal location, Southern-inspired dining in AG, a modern bar and steakhouse, and exceptional guest room and suite accommodations outfitted with classic elegance and style.

AG Steakhouse

AG brings a modern bar and steakhouse experience to downtown Atlanta. Leather booths and vintage photographs invite you to linger, while the menus showcase local and seasonal ingredients.
